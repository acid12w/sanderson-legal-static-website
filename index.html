Extensive Experience:

I have a wealth of experience in a wide array of legal subjects. However my areas of expertise are divorce, probate , and conveyancing matters. My satisfied clients can attest to the results I deliver. I have a Passion for Land Matters and a keen interest in land-related issues. I am passionate about helping individuals secure their ownership, rights, and interests in property.

People focused/ Personalized Attention:

I enjoy assisting my clients to find creative solutions to their problems, helping them achieve their desired life outcomes. I provide personalized services, ensuring that each client receives the attention and care they deserve. In addition to solving current problems, I also enjoy helping my clients to proactively plan and arrange their legal affairs to avoid potential problems. This includes services like wills, powers of attorney, and estate planning (excluding the creation of trusts).

Regular Communication:

I believe in maintaining open lines of communication with my clients. I am readily available via email and phone to discuss your matters.
